{{ ##BIO I am a Ph.D. candidate at the DBIIR Lab, Renmin University of China (RUC), supervised by Prof. Yueguo Chen. I visited Prof. Semih Salihoglu at the University of Waterloo in the last two years. My research focuses on query processing and storage optimization of analytical data management systems. Specifically, I work on extending RDBMSs to support hybrid graph-relational querying (GRainDB), developing a new graph database system (GraphflowDB), and investigating columnar caching and storage-layout optimization in data lakes (Pixels).
